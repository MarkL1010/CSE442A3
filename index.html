[A3: Marijuana usage in Washington State between 2016 and 2019]

Team Members: Mark Lei

Source: America Community Survey, 2016 - 2019. Washington State Department of Health, Marijuana and Tobacco Use.

Design Decisions

The reason why I chose this topic is because as an immigrant, it is very shocking for me to see Marijuana usage everywhere, as it is not legal in the country where I am from. I want to study the stats behind Marijuana usage in Washington State since it is legal here, and I was able to find some useful information in America Community Survey as well as Washington State Department of Health website. After I have my data resources set, I chose to display the data in different graphs, including maps, bar chart, pie chart, line chart, after that I applied different filters to them to make it as readable for readers as possible. As the data is not complicated after my heavy filtering, I am hoping my design decision make sense and can be easily understandable by readers.

Development Process

It is a solo project. After I have the idea in mind, it took me roughly an hour to figure out where to obtain a reliable source, and after I have the resources I need in hand it took me another 4-5 hours to finish up some data filtering, as the data provided was very detailed and it covers way too much information than what I need to display. After filtering, I spent another 3-4 hours on visual design and double checking the accuracy of my calculation and the data presentation. Lastly, I spent another hour or so to put it in html format for display purpose, as well as finishing up the writeup portion of this project. Thank you for your time!
